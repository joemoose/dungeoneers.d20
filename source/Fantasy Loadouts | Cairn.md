## Relics 

- **Arachnid Crest** _Web_ when in full darkness.
- **Eyestone** _Sense_ if placed in fresh water.
- **Holy Symbol** _Ward_ once per day following a devout prayer.
- **Talisman** _Wizard Mark_ when touching your quarry’s prints.
- **Unholy Symbol** _Hatred_ when bathed in blood.
- **Unicorn Horn** Blow to play a song that summons help. One use.
- **Witchwood Seal** _Beast Form_ when in clear view of the sun or moon.
- **Yew Wand**: *Pacify* when smacked on knuckles
