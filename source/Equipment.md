# Equipment

## **Coin and Currency**

The Shattered Lands is just one snapshot of many old, collapsed, and rebuilt eras. Currency has risen, fallen, changed, been forgotten, and salvaged by newcomers. The process repeats. As such, the working currency in this game is silver coins. 

## Rarity and Value

Villages, strongholds, and ports of call barter and trade based on the local rarity and value of any item or commodity. The GM rolls to determine the actual cost of any sought-after item based on the general guidelines below. If an item of interest is not listed here, determine its rarity or value accordingly.
