# Cutpurse

---

You live in the grey space between those who have power and those who don’t. You find opportunities where others see only chaos. With nimble fingers, you unburden both the richest merchant and the lowliest guard. 

---

## Starting Gear
 
- 3d6 coins
- Rations (3 uses)
- Torch (3 uses) 
- Twin Daggers (d6+d6, _bulky_)
- Padded Leather (1 Armor)
- Lockpicks
- Black Outfit (*small*)

## What was your last big job?

| d6 | Job |
| :-: | - |
| **1** | A noble's summer home. The place was full of fancy wine (+20 coins) but not much else. Take **Fence Cutters**. |
| **2** | A bank (you were caught). You bear a brand only visible by firelight, and anyone who sees the mark can ask you for a beer. Take **Retractable Wires**. |
| **3** | A guild warehouse. Take a **Ladder** (_bulky_, 10ft) and **Blinding Powder** (1 use). |
| **4** | Moneylender. Someone beat you to the job but left behind a **Scroll** of _Arcane Eye_ (*small*). |
| **5** | Constable's quarters. You escaped but left some friends behind. Take **Strong Silk Rope** (30ft) and a queasy feeling. |
| **6** | A university. You were seen but not pursued. You still don't know why. Take **Smoke Pellets** (3 uses). |
