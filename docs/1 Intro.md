TYROS – Players Guide (working title)
---

*TYROS*  
*Playtest Document v0.2, rules-lite edition*  
*February 19, 2023*  

TYROS is a rules-lite system for running old-school fantasy role-playing games.

- Tyros is inspired by Yochai Gal’s [Cairn](https://cairnrpg.com) and Ben Milton’s [Knave](https://questingbeast.itch.io/knave) and [Maze Rats](https://questingbeast.itch.io/maze-rats).
- Backgrounds adapted from [“20 Failed Careers”](https://aboleth-overlords.com/2021/03/22/20-failed-careers/), *Aboleth Overlords* by Justin Hamilton. 
- Spell recharge conditions adapted from [“D100 - Potential Spell Recharge Conditions”](https://blog.d4caltrops.com/2022/09/d100-potential-spell-recharge-conditions.html), *d4 Caltrops* by [Ktrey](https://twitter.com/ktrey).
