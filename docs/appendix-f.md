# Appendix F: Optional Character Classes

Consult the following table to determine your character’s class based on their starting HP. Your character’s class increases a starting attribute score and provides a unique ability.

| HP | Class | Ability |
| :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 3 | Wizard | Gain +1 WIL and you can drain your own Willpower atribute to add anima when casting a spell--one point of anima for each point of lost WIL. |
| 4 | Thief | Gain +1 DEX and advantage on lockpicking, sleight of hand, and stealth. |
| 5 | Fighter | Gain +1 STR and advantage on defending and attempting gambits in combat.
| 6 | Barbarian | Gain +1 LUCK and, when wearing no armor, increase your weapon’s damage die one step (d6 to d8, d8 to d10, etc.) |
