    Characters¶

    You're a newly minted DUNGEONEER: a tomb-raiding, treasure-seeking, door-kicking adventurer who wields a spellbook as readily as a blade. You're no ordinary folk. You'll live a dangerous life, questing for gold and glory. You'll delve into dark, forgotten places best left undisturbed. With a little luck, you might just retire before you die.

    Follow these steps to create your character.

    Dice Notation¶

    Before we start creating your character, here's a summary of the dice notation.

    Roll Description
    1d3 Roll one six-sided die, divide the result by two, and round up.
    1d6 Roll one six-sided die.
    2d6 Roll two six-sided dice and add the results.
    d66 Roll two six-sided dice, but instead of adding the results, read them in order to form a 2-digit number. For example, a 2 followed by a 4 is 24. A 1 followed by another 1 is 11.

    Step 3: Attributes¶

    You have three attributes that represent your physical and mental characteristics. Each attribute has a score, with higher scores indicating greater ability.

    • BRAWN: Actions requiring strength or constitution. A key attribute for warriors.
    • FINESSE: Actions requiring dexterity or charisma. A key attribute for rogues.
    • INTELLECT: Actions requiring intelligence or wisdom. A key attribute for magic.

    Assign the scores 3, 2, and 1 in any order you prefer.

    Example

    If you'd like to play a powerful character who's somewhat agile but not very bright, you might set your scores as follows: BRAWN 3, FINESSE 2, INTELLECT 1.

    Step 4: Hit Protection¶

    Hit protection (HP) is the amount of damage you can absorb before suffering potentially serious injuries. HP can be restored quickly and easily.

    Your HP equals 3 + BRAWN + FINESSE.

    The number of open slots in your inventory affects your HP.

    • Your HP increases by 1 for every 2 open slots in your inventory.
    • Your HP reduces to 0 if you have zero remaining open slots in your inventory.

    See Inventory Slots.

    Example

    If you have 2 BRAWN, 1 FINESSE, and 2 open inventory slots, you would have 3 + 2 + 1 + 1 = 7 HP.
