# Armor & Shields

A good way to survive a fight is by wearing proper protection.

Armor grants an Armor Rating (AR), which reduces incoming damage. A character can never have more than 6 AV, even if items would increase it above this number.

Wielding a shield increases AR but reduces Weapon Speed.

| :---- | :: | :---- | :--: |

| Medium | 2 | Requires Body 4+ | 60 |
| Heavy | 3 | Requires Body 6t | 120 |
