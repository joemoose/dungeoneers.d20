## Attribute Saves

When you describe your character taking a risky action with an essential but uncertain outcome, the GM may ask you to make an **attribute save** to determine whether you succeed. The GM will describe possible complications or outcomes to help inform your decision.

**To make an attribute save…**

Roll **d20** and add the applicable attribute score **modifier (MOD)** to the result:

- **If you roll 15 or higher**, you succeed without complications.
- **If you roll 10 to 14**, you succeed but with an unexpected complication.
- **If you roll 9 or lower**, you fail.

**Note**: With sufficient time, relevant skill or training, and the proper equipment, your actions will generally succeed—the GM won’t call for an attribute save. However, if any of these three requirements is missing, an attribute save is required to determine the outcome. If two or more are missing, the action is likely impossible. It’s time to think of a new approach.
