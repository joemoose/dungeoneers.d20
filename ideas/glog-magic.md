# Magic Hack

- You carry a **Grimoire** worth 300gp. It is *Bulky* and contains a single random spell.
- Treat any discovered Spellbooks as Scrolls.

## Casting Spells

1. Hold your **Grimoire** and choose a spell. The description may denote the spell's duration with **D** and range with **R**.
2. You have an amount of **Magic Dice** (d6) equal to the available inventory slots. Choose how many you wish to invest (up to 4). Spells will refer to these as [dice]. Some spells will refer to their [sum] as well.
3. Roll [dice]. For each die that shows a 4-6, you gain one *Fatigued* constraint.
4. If you roll 2 or more dice with the same result, something has gone very wrong. Add the matching dice values together and refer to the following **Mishaps** table for the result.

## **Scrolls**

Scrolls contain a single spell and are destroyed after a single use. Otherwise, they work like spells recorded in your **Grimoire**.

## **Copying Spells**

You may copy spells found in Scrolls to your **Grimoire**. It costs 50 coins for the gold inks and takes 6 hours to complete. The Scroll is destroyed in the process.

## **Creating a Grimoire**

Creating a **Grimoire** is time-consuming and expensive. You will need:

- A single Scroll, which is sacrificed through the process.
- 200 coins worth of ink, as well as a blank book.
- 6 hours of undisturbed labor in the light of a full moon. Afterward, you are *Deprived*.
- The spell contained within the Scroll becomes the first recorded spell.
