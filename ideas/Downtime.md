# Downtime

Downtime is the phase of play that happens between adventures. Downtime begins as soon as the party arrives back home after adventuring. During each downtime, characters may perform activities of daily living and run errands that can be quickly adjudicated on the spot.

Each player character may also perform 1 downtime action, such as the following example actions. Each action on this list takes time and is potentially significant in that it develops a character, leaves a mark on the campaign world, or provides a leg up for adventuring.

- **Building an Institution**: Institutions are any ongoing enterprise with fortunes that rise or fall. Examples include a teahouse, theater troupe, mercenary outfit, criminal gang, or guild.
- **Crafting**: Creating or enhancing functional and bespoke equipment.
- **Cultivating a Relationship**: A downtime activity that allows characters to forge or deepen a bond with an NPC. This can lead to unique storylines and unexpected benefits in future adventures.
- **Gathering Intelligence**: This downtime action allows a player to gather information from others.
- **Learning a Skill**: Characters will sometimes want to learn some skill, whether it’s a new language, sailing, becoming a card sharp, or the daunting art of riding giant sea creatures.
- **Martial Training**: There are two ways to engage in martial training: keeping in form and mastering a technique.
- **Researching**: This downtime action allows one to acquire lore or knowledge through study.
