# Battle Hardened

When an attack reduces your character's HP to exactly 0, they are scarred by the damage. Look up the resulting scar in the following table based on the damage taken. For example, if your character went from 4 HP to 0 HP, they would look at entry 4 (Broken Limb).

| Damage | Scar |
| :-: | :-- |
| 1 HP   | **Lasting Scar**: Roll 1d6 &#124; 1: Neck, 2: Hands, 3: Eye, 4: Chest, 5: Legs, 6: Ear. Roll 1d6. Take the new result if the total exceeds your max HP. |
| 2 HP | **Rattling Blow**: You’re disoriented and shaken. Describe how you refocus. Roll 1d6. Take the new result if the total exceeds your max HP. |
| 3 HP | **Walloped**: You’re sent flying and land flat on your face, winded. You are deprived until you rest for a few hours. Then, roll 1d6. Add that amount to your max HP. |
| 4 HP | **Broken Limb**: Roll 1d6 &#124; 1-2: Leg, 3-4: Arm, 5: Rib, 6: Skull. Once mended, roll 2d6. Take the new result if the total exceeds your max HP. |
| 5 HP | **Diseased**: You’re afflicted with a gross, uncomfortable infection. When you get over it, roll 2d6. Take the new result if the total exceeds your max HP. |
| 6 HP | **Reorienting Head Wound**: Roll 1d6 &#124; 1-2: STR, 3-4: DEX, 5-6: WIL. Roll 3d6. Take the new result if the total exceeds your ability's max score. |
| 7 HP | **Hamstrung**: You can barely move until you get serious help and rest. After recovery, Roll 1d6 &#124; 1-3: STR, 4-6: DEX. Roll 3d6. Take the new result if the total exceeds your ability's max score. |
| 8 HP | **Deafened**: You cannot hear anything until you find extraordinary aid. Regardless, make a WIL save. If you pass, increase your max WIL by 1d4. |
| 9 HP | **Re-brained**: Some hidden part of your psyche is knocked loose. Roll 3d6. Take the new result if the total exceeds your max WIL. |
| 10 HP | **Sundered**: An appendage is torn off, maimed, or useless. The Warden will tell you which. Then, make a WIL save. If you pass, increase your max WIL by 1d6. |
| 11 HP | **Mortal Wound**: You are deprived and out of action. You die in one hour unless healed. Upon recovery, roll 2d6. Take the new result if the total exceeds your max HP. |
| 12+ HP | **Doomed**: Death seemed so close, but somehow you survived. You die horribly if your next save against critical damage is a fail. If you pass, roll 3d6. Take the new result if the total is higher than your max HP. |
