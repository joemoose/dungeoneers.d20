## Class

Consult the table below to determine your character’s class based on their HP. Alternatively, roll a **d4** to choose one at random. Your class increases a starting **attribute score** and grants your character a unique **ability**.

| HP | Class | Ability |
| :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 3 | Wizard | Gain **+1 WIL** and **advantage** on **Willpower** checks. |
| 4 | Thief | Gain **+1 DEX** and **advantage** on **Lockpicking** and **Sleight of Hand**. |
| 5 | Fighter | Gain **+1 STR** and **advantage** on **Defending** and attempting **Gambits** in combat. |
| 6 | Barbarian | Gain **+1 LUCK** and, when wearing **no armor**, increase your **weapon’s damage die** by one step (d6 to d8, d8 to d10, etc.). |
