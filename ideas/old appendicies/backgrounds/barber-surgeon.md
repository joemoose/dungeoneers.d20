# Barber-Surgeon

---

You walk the line between healer and harrower, knowing the frailty of the flesh and the secrets that lay within. With the right tools, life, and death are merely words.

---

## Starting Gear

- 3d6 coins
- Rations (3 uses)
- Torch (3 uses) 
- Bonesaw (d4)
- Bandages (3 uses)
- Leech (restores 1 attribute loss, 3 uses)
- Stained Medical Finery (*small*)

## How have you "improved" yourself?

| d6 | Improvement |
| :-: | - |
| **1** | You have a replacement eye that magnifies objects, acts as a telescope, and provides minimal night vision. You cannot wear anything metal on your head, and the presence of strong magnets makes you *deprived*. |
| **2** | One foot is mostly metal (kick, d4), and you treat some tough terrain as **Easy**. Carry a **Oil Can** (6 uses). Use afterward or become *deprived* and noisey. |
| **3** | One of your fingers has been swapped, the bone replaced by gold and iron. Take a **Hook** and a **Screwdriver** that can attach to the fingertip. |
| **4** | Both ears have been surgically enhanced, tripling your hearing. You can *focus on a specific sound* at a great distance, such as a conversation. You wear an ear flap to protect against sudden loud noises (WIL save to avoid temporary paralysis). |
| **5** | Your chest is lined with alchemical sigils, *toughening the skin* (+1 Armor). |
| **6** | One arm is fully metal (fist, d6), and comes off at the shoulder (*bulky* when carried). It can move independently if you are within sight of it. |

| d6 | Item | Description |
| :-: | - | - |

| **2** | **Graftgrub** | A small worm that can fuse inanimate objects with parts of the body. 1 use. |
