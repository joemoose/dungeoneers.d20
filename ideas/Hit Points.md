## Step 5: Hit Points

Hit points (HP) represent the damage you can shrug off before suffering any physical harm. HP is easily and quickly restored.

Add your four attribute modifiers, **STR**, **DEX**, **WIL**, and **LUCK**, to determine your base hit points to a minimum of zero.

Your base HP is then increased by 1 point for every two open slots in your inventory.

### Inventory and HP

Add **1 point** to your base HP for every **open slot** in your inventory.

If you carry a full inventory (i.e., filling all 12 slots), your HP is reduced to **zero**.
